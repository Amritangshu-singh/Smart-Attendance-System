Project Information

Project Title: Smart Attendance System

The Smart Attendance System leverages advanced facial recognition technology to automate and streamline the attendance marking process. This innovative solution significantly enhances the accuracy and efficiency of attendance management in educational institutions. By utilizing a simple group photo, the system can recognize individual students and automatically mark their attendance, reducing manual errors and saving valuable time for educators and students alike.
